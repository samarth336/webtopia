Computer Science Engineering (CSE)

CSE deals with computing, programming, algorithms, AI, and data structures.
It is one of the most sought-after fields due to its vast career
opportunities in software development, cybersecurity, and AI.

Mechanical Engineering (ME)

Mechanical Engineering focuses on the design, production,
and operation of machines, engines, and mechanical systems.

Civil Engineering (CE)

Civil Engineering focuses on infrastructure development,
including roads, bridges, buildings, and water supply systems.

Electrical Engineering (EE)

JEE deals with the study of electrical circuits, systems, and power generation.

Electronics & Communication Engineering (ECE)

ECE covers communication systems, embedded systems, and semiconductor technology.
